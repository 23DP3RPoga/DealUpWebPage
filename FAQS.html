Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions

What is DealUp?

DealUp is a buy-and-sell marketplace that allows users to post ads for items they want to sell and find great deals nearby.

Is DealUp free to use?

Yes! Posting ads and browsing listings is completely free. However, we offer premium ad boosts to increase visibility for a small fee.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up using your email, phone number, or social media. Just download the app and follow the registration steps!

Buying & Selling

How do I post an ad?

  • Tap the "Create Listing" button.
  • Upload clear photos of your item.
  • Add a title, description, price, and category.
  • Choose your location and post the ad!

How do I find items to buy?

  • Use the search bar to find specific items.
  • Browse through categories for the best deals.
  • Use filters to refine results by price, condition, and location.

Safety & Security

How do I stay safe when buying or selling?

  • Meet in public places (e.g., malls, police stations, cafes).
  • Inspect the item before paying.
  • Avoid sending money upfront to unknown sellers.
  • Report suspicious users via the app.
