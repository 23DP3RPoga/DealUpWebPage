Help Center

Welcome to the DealUp Help Center!

Find answers to common questions, troubleshooting guides, and tips on how to use DealUp effectively.

1. Getting Started

How do I create an account

  • Download the DealUp app from Google Play or the App Store
  • Click Sign Up and register with your email, phone number, or social media
  • Verify your account through the confirmation email or SMS.

How do I log in

  • Open the app and enter your registered email or phone number
  • If you forgot your password, use the “Forgot Password” option.

2. Buying & Selling

How do I post an ad

  • Tap “Post an Ad” on the homepage
  • Upload clear photos of your item
  • Add a title, description, price, and category
  • Set your location and post the ad.

How do I find an item to buy

  • Use the search bar to find specific products
  • Browse by categories
  • Apply filters like price, location, and condition.

How do I contact a seller

  • Click “Message” on an ad to start a chat with the seller
  • Avoid sharing personal or payment details outside the app.

3. Payments & Promotions

How can I promote my ad

  • Use our Boost Ad feature for better visibility
  • Choose from Featured Listings or Urgent Tags.

What payment methods do you accept

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and in-app payments.

Can I get a refund for a promoted ad

Refunds are subject to our Refund Policy. Contact support@dealup.com for more details.

4. Safety & Security

How do I stay safe when buying or selling

  • Meet in public places like shopping malls or police stations
  • Never send money before receiving the item
  • Verify the item’s condition before making a payment.

How do I report a scam or fraud

  • Use the “Report” button on an ad or user profile
  • Contact support@dealup.com if you suspect fraudulent activity.

5. Account & Settings

  • To edit/delete your ad: Go to My Listings, select the ad, and choose Edit or Delete.
  • To deactivate/delete account: Go to Settings > Account and follow the steps.
  • To reset your password: Use the “Forgot Password” option.
